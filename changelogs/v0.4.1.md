# Version 0.4.1 - 2026-01-30

## Fixed

### Issue: Incorrect Badge Color Assignment for Series Searches

- **Problem**: When searching for a series name (e.g., "Harry Potter"), the system was converting it to the first book's full title (e.g., "Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone"), causing incorrect badge color assignments
- **Root Cause**: Title correction step was transforming series names into specific book titles, changing user intent from "series information" to "specific book information"
- **Impact**:
  - Cream badge (original search query) was being assigned to the first book instead of the series concept
  - Series searches were treated as standalone book searches
  - Badge colors didn't accurately reflect what the user originally searched for
- **Solution**: Changed title correction logic to preserve search intent:
  - Series names stay as series names: "harry potter" → "Harry Potter" (not converted to first book title)
  - Standalone books stay as standalone: "48 lows of power" → "The 48 Laws of Power"
  - Only spelling and capitalization are corrected, not the nature of the search
  - Green badges now correctly highlight individual book mentions in responses
  - Cream badge accurately reflects the user's original search intent
- **Result**: More accurate and intuitive badge coloring that matches user expectations
- **Testing**: Verified with series searches ("Harry Potter", "Lord of the Rings") and standalone books ("48 Laws of Power", "1984")
